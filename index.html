FUN FACT
Did you know California is home to the world's tallest tree, a coast redwood named Hyperion. Hyperion stands in the Redwood National Park and reaches a staggering height of over 379 feet (115.7 meters). This magnificent tree was discovered in 2006, and its exact location is kept secret to help protect it. The towering height of Hyperion showcases the awe-inspiring natural wonders that California has to offer.
